# 🔊 EPIC 4 — Story 4.2 Section Segmentation

## 🎯 Goal  
Identify the **structural sections of the song** (e.g., intro, verse, chorus, bridge) using audio features and repetition patterns.

---

## 🛠️ Tools

- Essentia  
  → feature extraction (loudness, spectral features, HPCP)

- Librosa (optional)  
  → novelty detection, self-similarity

- Algorithms:
  - Self-Similarity Matrix (SSM)
  - Novelty Curve Detection

---

## ⚙️ Tasks

### 1. Feature Aggregation (Input Preparation)

Use outputs from:
- EPIC 2 → harmonic (HPCP / chords)
- EPIC 4.1 → energy features

Aggregate per beat/bar:
- loudness
- spectral centroid
- spectral flux
- onset strength
- chroma (HPCP)

---

### 2. Similarity Analysis

- Compute Self-Similarity Matrix (SSM)
- Compare feature vectors across time
- Detect repeating patterns:
  - diagonal blocks → repeated sections

---

### 3. Boundary Detection

- Compute novelty curve from SSM
- Detect peaks → candidate section boundaries

---

### 4. Section Labeling (Heuristic Phase)

Assign labels using rules:

#### Intro
- Low energy
- Appears only at beginning
- No repetition

#### Verse
- Moderate energy
- Repeated pattern
- Lower intensity than chorus

#### Chorus
- Highest energy
- Strong repetition
- High onset density

#### Bridge
- Low similarity with previous sections
- Transitional

---

### 5. Temporal Alignment

- Snap boundaries to:
  - nearest bar
  - or beat

---

### 6. Confidence Scoring

- Assign confidence score based on:
  - repetition strength
  - feature consistency

---

---

## 🧪 Acceptance Criteria

- Sections align with human perception (±1–2 bars tolerance)
- Chorus sections:
  - correctly identified
  - repeated consistently
- Boundaries:
  - align with bars
  - do not cut mid-phrase
- Avoid over-segmentation

---

## ⚠️ Common Pitfalls

- Using only energy → misses harmonic repetition
- Ignoring repetition → fails to detect chorus
- Too many segments → noisy output

---

## 🎯 Key Insight

Sections are **lighting intent zones**:

- Intro → minimal lighting, slow build
- Verse → groove, subtle motion
- Chorus → full intensity, movement, strobes
- Bridge → contrast / transition

👉 This layer bridges **audio analysis → show design**
