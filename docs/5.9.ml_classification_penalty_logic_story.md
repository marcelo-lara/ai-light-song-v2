# EPIC 5 - Story 5.9
## ML Classification with Physical-Constraint Penalty Logic

## Goal
Implement the training and inference-time penalty logic for the ML event classifier so predictions remain grounded in measurable audio transients rather than unsupported structural guesses.

## Intent

This story owns how the model is penalized or rewarded during training and inference. It does not own downstream lighting behavior; it produces penalty and alignment metadata that later stories may consume.

## Core Logic: The Penalty Function
1. **Transient Penalty Logic**:
   - During training/inference, if the model predicts a "Hard Hit" or "Section Boundary" at a timestamp where the `bass_att` or `spectral_flux` is below the local moving average, apply a high penalty score to the loss/confidence.
2. **Neural Signature "Sanity Check"**:
   - Use the "FFT Boundary Audit" (Story 4.5) as a secondary validation. The model is "rewarded" in its alignment score if its predicted transitions align within 50ms of a physical sub-band spike.

## Processing Steps
1. **Contextual Windowing**: Feed the model a "Contextual Window" (e.g., 5 seconds) that includes the volume history and attenuated transients as primary input features.
2. **Loss Function Modification**: Integrate the Physical-Constraint Loss into the backpropagation loop.
3. **Penalty Export**: Persist event-level or frame-level penalty metadata so debugger surfaces can show where physical evidence strengthened or weakened a prediction.
4. **Reproducibility**: Ensure the **Mersenne Twister (MT19937)** seed is visible in the logs and metadata so penalized scenarios can be reproduced exactly during tuning.

## Acceptance Criteria
- The training or inference pipeline exports penalty metadata that can drive a debugger penalty heatmap or severity lane.
- Model alignment with physical transients (within 50ms) improves by at least 20% compared to a non-penalized baseline.
- The exported metadata makes it explicit which predictions were penalized, rewarded, or left unchanged by the physical-constraint logic.

## Output Artifacts
- `models/event_classifier/penalty_logic_metadata.json`
- `data/artifacts/<Song - Artist>/event_inference/penalty_timeline.json`

## Validation

- Confirm repeated runs with the same MT19937 seed produce identical penalty metadata.
- Confirm penalty exports can be joined back to the related ML prediction windows without guessing.
- Confirm downstream consumers can reduce confidence or aggressiveness based on the exported metadata without re-implementing the penalty logic.

## Out of Scope

- Fixture-specific intensity policy
- Debugger rendering details
